Our story

MeatUthere is more than a business. It’s where the creativity and all the great tastes meet. A team of 3 young adults decided to take their family’s vision to the next level. And they finally succeded! It’s not only about the food, it’s about diversity and coziness, a place you feel you belong to.

Meet us here and you won’t regret it. We want this place to be the restaurant where you laugh the most with your friends or you have dinner with your family members that you haven’t seen in a long time. Make MeatUthere part of your journey.

Taste from our delicious dishes and enjoy your time with your loved ones. We will keep you updated with our events and also with our flexible timetable. Anything for our clients so they can enjoy their time here.
